FAQ

What is Well It?

Well It is a Machine Learning based toolkit which can be used to predict wheather construction of a well is feasable at a particular location or not

What is the purpose of Well It?

To avoid wastage of human and natural resources by using Machine Learning

Who can use Well It?

Government and Local construction agencies and even citizens can use this.

What data is used for evaluation of the results?

Well-It works on the available data provided by the Government Census

What is the advantage of using Well-It?

Well-It can predict the feasable place for well construction and also the approximate amount to be spent

Can we trust the information provided by this tool?

As this works on machine learning model,100% prediction is not guarananteed, yet it evaluates to an accurate extent
